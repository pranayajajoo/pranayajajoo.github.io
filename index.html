About

I am a Master’s student in Technology Management (2019-20) at the Gies College of Business, University 0f Illinois Urbana-Champaign. I am currently working as a Product Management Intern at Runaway , wheren I have developed a detailed product roadmap for developing a web application for a mental health initiative, and conducting weekly agile sprints for all stages of the SDLC based on user stories and monitoring product iterations. During my time at UIUC, I have been focused on working in various consulting projects and case competitions. Prior to this, I received my Bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering from the Department of Electrical Engineering at the Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute, Mumbai.
